Vinland Saga

Vinland Saga is a manga series by Makoto Yukimura. It was serialized in Weekly Shōnen Magazine from April 2005 before moving to Monthly Afternoon between December 2005 and July 2025, and is published in tankōbon format by Kōdansha. The French version has been published by Kurokawa since January 2009.

Dorohedoro

Dorohedoro is a seinen manga by Q Hayashida. It was serialized in Japan in Monthly Ikki magazine between 2000 and 2014, then in Hibana magazine between 2015 and 2017, before concluding in Monthly Shōnen Sunday between November 2017 and September 2018.

Attack on titan

Shingeki no Kyojin, also known by its English title Attack on Titan, is a shōnen manga written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. It was serialized between September 2009 and April 2021 in Bessatsu Shōnen Magazine, then compiled into thirty-four tankōbon volumes by publisher Kodansha.
